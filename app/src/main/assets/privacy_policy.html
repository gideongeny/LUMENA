Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Lumena Music Player ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

2. Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. All data (music files, playlists, preferences) is stored locally on your device.

Permissions We Request:

  • READ_MEDIA_AUDIO / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: To display and play your music files.
  • INTERNET: To fetch lyrics (from LRCLIB), metadata (from MusicBrainz), and search/stream online content (via YouTube/NewPipe).

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services for specific features:

  • LRCLIB: We send track title and artist to LRCLIB to retrieve synchronized lyrics. LRCLIB Privacy
  • MusicBrainz: We send track metadata to retrieve album art and details. MusicBrainz Privacy
  • YouTube (via NewPipe Extractor): Search queries are sent to YouTube to find videos. No personal account data is accessed.

4. Kids Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

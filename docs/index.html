Meet the Creator

Hi, I'm Gideon Geny. My vision for Lumena was simple: to build the most fluid and beautiful music player for Android.

In a world of subscription services, I wanted to create a home for your local library that feels premium, modern, and personal. I believe software should be fast, respect your privacy, and look stunning. Lumena is my contribution to that ideal.
